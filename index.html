MEET: Death

There is no justice; There is only me, Death

Death is the Disc's personification of death. The very few people that actually saw him described him as "A tall skeletal figure with a black robe and a scythe, galaxies in eyes".

Only people of magic can see Death, everyone else "felt like there was someone extra in the room, one who caused existential dread, I just coouldn't remember their appearance."

"It's because your brain beleives that you're not supposed to see Death whilst you're alive, and so your brain refused to notice him," the wizards of the Unseen University explained.
